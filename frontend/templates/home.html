Our Mission

Imagine you're driving your electric vehicle (EV) and, instead of worrying about where to charge next or if you're overpaying for electricity, your EV smartly navigates you to the optimal charging station in real-time. Our AI-agent based EV charging optimization and arbitration system does just that. It not only helps EV owners save money on charging costs but also contributes to improving grid stability. By intelligently distributing charging demand across available infrastructure, it prevents overloads and promotes the use of renewable energy sources. Simplify your EV charging, save money, and be a part of the green revolution with our innovative solution.
